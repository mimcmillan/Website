Justifying Bayes’ Theorem

One type of justification is that if a gambler updates his or her degrees of belief using Bayes’ Theorem and the axioms of probability, he or she will avoid a “Dutch Book”, i.e. a collection of bets that leads to sure losses. However, it is not necessary to follow Bayesian updating to avoid Dutch Books consistently.
